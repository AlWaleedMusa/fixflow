Smart Pricing Calculator

The smartest pricing calculator based on device type and repair complexity. Never undercharge again.

🚀
Quick Start Guide: Pricing & Estimation System
  1. Initialization: Click "New Pricing Estimate" to generate a new calculation workspace.
  2. Device Category: Select hardware type — system adjusts Hourly Labor Rate automatically.
  3. Complexity Level: Assess difficulty (Easy / Medium / Complex) to calibrate risk and precision.
  4. Spare Parts Cost: Enter the net purchase price of the replacement component.
  5. Output: System calculates 3 strategic pricing tiers — Premium, Competitive, Wholesale.

CRM (Customer Relationship Management)

Your customer database is your shop's greatest asset — track repair history, maximize lifetime value, and turn every fix into a lasting relationship.

🚀
Quick Start Guide: Customer Management & Loyalty
  1. ❄️ Engagement Status: If a customer is inactive for 90+ days, the system auto-triggers an ❄️ Inactive Customer alert — send a re-engagement offer.
  2. 📲 WhatsApp Outreach: Use "Inspection Offer" or "Discount Offer" buttons to send pre-written marketing messages instantly.
  3. 🎫 Ticket Management: Use "Create Ticket" to open a new repair record directly linked to the customer profile.
  4. 🏆 Loyalty Points: Auto-calculated — every 10 EGP = 1 point. Tiers: 🆕 New · 🌟 Pro (500pts) · 💎 Elite (1000pts) · 👑 VIP (1500pts)
